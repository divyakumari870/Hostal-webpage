Frequently Asked Questoins(FAQs)
Is the Hostel building within the campus?

Yes,Both Old and New Hostel Building is within the campus at a walking distance from our college.

what is the process of allotment of seat?

Allotment is on the first cum first serve basis,usually,when the number of applicants are more than the number of hostel seats,a waiting list is drawn up and aiiocation is as per the list and availability arising.

How many meals are provided?

Four meals,which includes Breakfast,Lunch,Evening snacks and Dinner.

Does the mess follow a food chart?

Yes,it follows the food chart for Breakfast,Lunch,Evening Snacks and Dinner.

Are parents informed,if student leaves hostel campus after 9 pm or if she wants to go home for few days?

Yes,when the student submits the application to the concerned person -in charge of hostel,the hostel in charge calls parents to communicate the same.
