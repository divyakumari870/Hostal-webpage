After entering in hostel mess process:

  • First you have to paid the hostel fee which is 8000.
  • Then you will have to go to the mess incharge with that receipt and submit one month's extra fee of the mess.
  • They will give you a mess fee receipt & register your name in their register book.
  • Once your name registered in the mess ragister book the a "plate & katori" will be issue to you.
  • Now you will be a part of mess & visible to eat in the mess.
  • Now every month's you have to submit your mess fee which is 2300.
  • This facility is also available that if there is a holiday in any month, then you will be charged the same amount of money as the day is fed.
  • Timing for eating in the mess is:
    • Breakfast: 8:30am-9:30am
    • Lunch: 1:00pm- 2:30pm
    • Dinner: 7:30pm- 8:30pm
  • At the time of no dues in the last, you will not have to pay the money of the last month because you had submitted one month's extra fee in your starting.
