Hostel Discipline:

  • The hostellers are required to abide by the hostel rules.

  • on going outside the hostel, by 6:00pm, you will have to come back to the hostel in any case.
  • Drinking & smoking not strictly allowed, for this you can also get the harshest punishment.
  • Any disturbing things like loud music, party not allowed.
  • you can go outside of the hostel only Once in a week
